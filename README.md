# First Principles · 第一性原理

A bilingual (English / 简体中文) thinking engine for **First Principles Thinking** — the discipline of breaking any problem down to its irreducible truths and rebuilding the answer from the ground up. A fusion of philosophy, physics, cognitive science, engineering, and AI research, designed as a lifelong intellectual tool rather than a normal web page.

## What's inside

- **The Method** — the canonical six-step descend-and-rebuild loop.
- **Theory** — what a first principle is, the ontology of knowledge, the epistemology of reasoning, and interactive comparisons of first-principles vs. analogy vs. heuristics.
- **Lineage** — an interactive timeline from Aristotle → Euclid → Descartes → Newton → Lavoisier → Einstein → Musk.
- **Applications** — four worked case studies (rocket cost, battery cost, AI model simplification, personal decisions), each shown as a decomposition tree.
- **The Decomposition Engine** — type any problem; get back assumptions, base truths, and a reconstruction, visualized as a tree, a layered map, or a logic flow. Export to JSON, PNG, or a printable PDF report.
- **First-Principles Capacity** — a 7-axis radar meta-model with live sliders.
- **Knowledge Library** — long-form bilingual readings, philosophy to cognitive science.
- **Thinking Space** — a community board to publish decompositions, vote on the most fundamental / most elegant, and comment by reasoning layer (saved locally in your browser).

Full real-time EN ⇄ 中文 language toggle with no page reload.
